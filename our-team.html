Meet the Team

Get to know our family

Chirag Patel

Owner/Managing Director

As Managing Director, Chirag oversees various aspects of the portfolio including analysis and selection of future development opportunities, financial structuring and new construction/renovation management. Prior to founding Pathfinder, he was with the private equity firm American Securities Capital Partners and at the consultancy McKinsey & Co. in the Corporate Finance & Strategy practice. Chirag received a Bachelor of Science magna cum laude from the Wharton School of Business at the University of Pennsylvania.

Rahul Bahl

Owner/Managing Director

As Managing Director, Rahul oversees multiple aspects of the portfolio including revenue generation strategies, implementation of core operational and Human Resources strategies, analysis and selection of future development opportunities and also oversees interior design for new properties and properties undergoing renovations. Prior to founding Pathfinder, Rahul received a Masters in Professional Accounting from the University of Texas and a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from Trinity University.

Danielle Schneider

Vice-President

As Vice President, Danielle oversees all sales and marketing efforts at Pathfinder hotels. Her leadership and competitive spirit help foster a sales and guest driven culture at each hotel. In addition to leadership in sales, Danielle is passionate about training and development of team members and spearheads the effort, along with David Rojas, to help Pathfinder develop its team members to their fullest career potential. Prior to joining Pathfinder, Danielle was the Vice-President of Sales at CL Hospitality where she spearheaded the sales efforts of portfolio of hotels in the Midwest. Danielle received a Bachelor of Arts in Business and Hospitality Management from Eastern Illinois University.

David Rojas

Vice-President

David joined Pathfinder in 2012 as the General Manager of the Staybridge Suites NASA/Houston. A year later, he was promoted to his current role as Vice-President. In this role, David ensures guest rooms, guest suites and lobbies at Pathfinder hotels are maintained in pristine fashion and that guests are consistently enjoying an outstanding experiences . David also ensures Pathfinder hotels are operating efficiently & along with Danielle Schneider leads the company’s thorough training & development platform. Prior to joining Pathfinder, David was the Area Operations Manager for Extended Stay Hotels in Houston, Texas.

Katelyn Galbreath

Administrator/Human Resources

As the company administrator for Pathfinder, Katelyn supports individual hotels in executing company-wide initiatives. She ensures the sales, operations, and human resources functions at each hotel are following protocols and best practices. Katie is passionate about Pathfinder remaining true to its cultural values and helps recruit talent which fits the company’s culture and vision. Prior to joining the company, Katelyn worked for White Lodging. Katelyn received a Bachelor of Science in Hospitality and Tourism Management from Purdue University, Calumet.

Leah Solomon

Senior Accountant

Leah reviews the day to day financial transactions of Pathfinder's portfolio and helps prepare financial reports for each property. She provides the critical analysis to ensure our properties are armed with insightful data which helps the company make good business decisions. Prior to joining Pathfinder, Leah served as a Controller for a regional insurance company and as the assistant Controller of a educational institution. Leah is a certified public accountant and graduated with Honors from Touro College with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting.

Blake Lindsay

Revenue Manager

Blake joined Pathfinder in 2011 as a Sales and Service Agent at the Staybridge Suites NASA/Houston. Blake worked at multiple positions within the hotel, finishing out his tenure at the Staybridge Suites as the Front Office Manager in 2013. He was then promoted to the Assistant General Manager of the brand new Holiday Inn Express & Suites in Austin, Texas where he played a key role in the opening as well as daily operations related to running the hotel. In March of 2015, Blake was promoted to his current role as Revenue Manager where he is responsible for both strategic and tactical initiatives related to revenue maximization for all of the hotels within the Pathfinder portfolio.

Mary Birgen

Accounting Clerk

Mary graduated from Austin Community College with an Associate Degree as an Accounting Technician. In her role as Accounting Clerk for Pathfinder, she is responsible for ensuring the monthly mortage payment, management and franchise fees, cash reconciliations and accounts payable are coded correctly for each hotel.
